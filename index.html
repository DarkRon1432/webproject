Favorite Anime

Solo Leveling

Solo Leveling, also alternatively translated as Only I Level Up, is a South Korean portal fantasy web novel written by Chugong. It was serialized in Kakao's digital comic and fiction platform KakaoPage beginning on July 25, 2016, and was later published by D&C Media under their Papyrus label since November 4, 2016.

The Breaker

Chunwoo, the last user of the Black Heaven and Earth Techniques, joins the Black Forest Defense group to avenge his master's death. He infiltrates Seoul to complete a mission and teaches martial arts to Shiwoon Yi. Chunwoo's lover, Shiho Lee, is killed by the Martial Arts Alliance, which further fuels his hatred.

Lookism

Daniel Park is an unpopular and bullied high school student who moves to Seoul to start over. Before he begins school, he wakes up in a new body that is tall, muscular, and handsome. He can switch between the two bodies, with the handsome one appearing when he's awake and the original when he's sleeping.
